## Sensitive data guidance

Do not paste confidential, personal, regulated, customer, employee, health, financial, legal, or proprietary data into unapproved AI tools.

AI pilots should minimize data collection and avoid sensitive inputs unless the organization has completed qualified privacy, security, and legal review.
