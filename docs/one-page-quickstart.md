# One-Page Quickstart

## Responsible AI Adoption Review

Use this before piloting AI in a business workflow.

### 1. Define the use case

What workflow changes? What AI does what? Who owns it?

Template: `01-ai-use-case-intake.md`

### 2. Identify affected people

Who uses it? Who is affected by it? Could it change job quality, access, fairness, privacy, or trust?

Templates: `02-stakeholder-and-impact-map.md`, `04-workforce-impact-checklist.md`

### 3. Measure value

What is the baseline? What improvement is expected? How will quality and rework be measured?

Template: `03-productivity-roi-measurement-plan.md`

### 4. Register risk

What can go wrong? Who owns the risk? What controls are required?

Template: `05-risk-register.md`

### 5. Keep humans accountable

What decisions stay human-owned? Who reviews AI output? Who can override it?

Template: `06-human-oversight-plan.md`

### 6. Pilot narrowly

Use limited scope, limited data, and clear stop conditions.

Guide: `implementation-guide.md`

### 7. Review before scale

Stop, revise, continue, expand, or scale only after evidence review.

Template: `08-pilot-review-scorecard.md`
