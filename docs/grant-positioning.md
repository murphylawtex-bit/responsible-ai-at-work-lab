# Grant Positioning for the Toolkit

## Working initiative names

- AI Adoption & Society Lab
- Responsible AI at Work Institute
- Applied AI Impact Lab

## Short description

The Responsible AI Adoption Toolkit for SMEs is an open-source framework that helps small and mid-sized organizations evaluate, pilot, and govern AI use cases while measuring productivity, workforce impact, job quality, transparency, and societal risk.

## Grant thesis

AI adoption is moving faster than most small and mid-sized organizations can responsibly govern. Large enterprises can hire specialized AI governance teams, but smaller organizations often lack practical tools to evaluate ROI, protect workers, manage risk, and communicate AI use transparently. This toolkit translates responsible AI principles into simple templates, pilot workflows, and measurement practices that SMEs can use immediately.

## Why fund it

- Bridges the gap between high-level AI ethics frameworks and day-to-day business adoption.
- Supports responsible productivity gains without ignoring workers and affected communities.
- Produces open-source public-good materials.
- Can generate research evidence about how AI affects real workflows, job quality, and organizational decision-making.
- Scales through templates, guides, training, and case studies.

## Likely funder fits

- Responsible AI / trustworthy AI programs
- Future-of-work foundations
- Open-source and civic-tech funders
- Public-interest technology programs
- AI workforce/adoption research grants
- SME innovation and productivity initiatives
