Responsible AI at Work Lab

Practical open tools helping small and mid-sized organizations adopt AI responsibly while measuring productivity, workforce impact, human oversight, and governance risk.

View the toolkit on GitHub Quickstart NIST AI RMF Crosswalk

Why this exists

AI adoption is entering ordinary work faster than many organizations can govern it. Large enterprises can hire AI governance teams; small and mid-sized organizations usually cannot. This project translates responsible-AI principles into workflow-level tools operators can actually use.

What the toolkit helps answer

Should we use AI here?

Define the workflow, AI role, users, affected people, and intended outcome.

What value is real?

Measure baseline productivity, quality, rework, review burden, and user experience.

Who is affected?

Map workforce impact, job quality, autonomy, transparency, fairness, and stakeholder feedback.

Who remains accountable?

Define human oversight, review rules, override paths, escalation, and stop conditions.
