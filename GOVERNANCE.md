Responsible AI at Work Lab is currently an early-stage independent public-interest project.

## Decision principles

Project decisions should favor:

1. Practical usefulness for small and mid-sized organizations.
2. Human accountability and meaningful oversight.
3. Worker and stakeholder impact, not only productivity.
4. Open, inspectable, reusable materials.
5. Clear limitations and avoidance of overclaiming.

## Contribution review

Contributions are reviewed for:

- clarity
- operational usefulness
- risk awareness
- alignment with the toolkit purpose
- avoidance of legal/compliance overclaiming

## Current maturity

This is an early public draft. Governance will evolve if contributors, users, or funders become involved.
