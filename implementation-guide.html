30-Day Pilot Guide

Run a limited, measurable AI pilot with human accountability and real feedback before scale-up.

HomeQuickstartExamples
Week 1

Define and screen

Pick one narrow workflow. Complete use-case intake, impact map, and initial risk register.

Week 2

Measure and govern

Set baselines, define human oversight, brief affected workers/users, and approve pilot conditions.

Weeks 3–4

Pilot

Run limited scope. Track time, quality, rework, review burden, incidents, and feedback.

End

Review

Complete the scorecard and decision memo. Stop, revise, expand, or scale with controls.

Good pilot traits

  • Narrow enough to reverse.
  • Clear human owner.
  • Baseline exists.
  • No high-impact decisions without special review.
  • Workers/users can give feedback.
  • Risks are tracked visibly.
