Toolkit mapping

MapUse-case intake, stakeholder map, workforce checklistUnderstand context, affected people, workflow changes, and intended/possible impacts.
MeasureROI plan, risk register, pilot scorecardCompare results against baseline and track risk likelihood, impact, controls, and evidence quality.
ManageRisk register, oversight plan, implementation guidePrioritize mitigations, assign owners, monitor risks, and decide whether to stop/revise/scale.

SME scoring model

Use a 1–5 score across seven dimensions. The point is not false precision; it is forcing useful conversations before AI becomes infrastructure.

28–35

Pilot likely appropriate with normal controls.

20–27

Revise before pilot; address weak dimensions.

Below 20

Do not pilot until governance and risk gaps are fixed.

Special review: Employment, pay, safety, legal, financial, health, housing, education, access rights, surveillance, children, vulnerable populations, or protected classes require extra review regardless of score.
