# Contributing

This toolkit is intended to become a practical open resource for responsible AI adoption in small and mid-sized organizations.

## Useful contributions

- Better plain-language templates
- Example AI pilot reviews
- Worker-feedback questions
- Risk/control examples
- Sector-specific adaptations
- NIST AI RMF or other standards mappings
- Accessibility improvements
- Translation/localization

## Contribution principles

1. Keep it practical for non-enterprise organizations.
2. Avoid vague ethics language without an operational action.
3. Preserve human accountability.
4. Include worker and stakeholder impact, not only productivity.
5. Prefer simple, inspectable materials over complex proprietary tooling.

## Review standard

A contribution should help an SME answer at least one of these questions:

- Should we use AI for this workflow?
- Who is affected?
- What could go wrong?
- Who remains accountable?
- How do we measure whether it worked?
- What conditions must be met before scale-up?
