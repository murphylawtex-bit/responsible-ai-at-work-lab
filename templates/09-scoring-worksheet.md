## Decision guidance

- **28–35:** Pilot likely appropriate with normal controls.
- **20–27:** Revise before pilot; address weak dimensions.
- **Below 20:** Do not pilot until governance and risk gaps are fixed.

## Override rules

Regardless of score, require special review if the use case affects:

- hiring, firing, discipline, pay, promotion, or scheduling;
- health, safety, legal, financial, lending, housing, insurance, or education outcomes;
- access to essential services;
- surveillance, monitoring, or automated evaluation of people;
- children, vulnerable populations, or protected classes.
