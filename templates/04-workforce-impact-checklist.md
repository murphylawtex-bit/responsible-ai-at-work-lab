## Job and task impact

- [ ] Tasks automated or partially automated are documented.
- [ ] Tasks requiring human judgment are documented.
- [ ] New skills/training requirements are identified.
- [ ] Potential role redesign is described.
- [ ] Potential headcount/hour/pay impacts are disclosed internally before scale.

## Worker participation

- [ ] Affected workers have reviewed the proposed workflow.
- [ ] Feedback channels are available before and during pilot.
- [ ] Workers can report harmful, inaccurate, or burdensome AI behavior.
- [ ] Pilot results include worker feedback, not only management metrics.

## Guardrails

- [ ] AI outputs are not used as sole basis for hiring, firing, discipline, pay, promotion, or scheduling decisions.
- [ ] Workers know when AI is being used in the workflow.
- [ ] Workers know how to challenge or correct AI output.
- [ ] Human review is meaningful, not rubber-stamping.
- [ ] Training is provided before expectations change.
