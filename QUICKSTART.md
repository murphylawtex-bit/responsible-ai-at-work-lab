# Quickstart: Run a Responsible AI Pilot Review

## Step 1 — Define the use case

Complete `templates/01-ai-use-case-intake.md`.

If the use case is vague, stop and narrow it.

## Step 2 — Map affected people

Complete `templates/02-stakeholder-and-impact-map.md` and `templates/04-workforce-impact-checklist.md`.

If affected workers or users cannot provide feedback, do not scale the pilot.

## Step 3 — Measure before changing the workflow

Complete `templates/03-productivity-roi-measurement-plan.md`.

If there is no baseline, productivity claims are guesses.

## Step 4 — Register risks

Complete `templates/05-risk-register.md`.

Every high-impact risk needs an owner, mitigation, and review date.

## Step 5 — Define human oversight

Complete `templates/06-human-oversight-plan.md`.

If nobody is accountable when AI is wrong, the pilot is not ready.

## Step 6 — Run a limited pilot

Use a narrow workflow, limited data, and clear stop conditions.

## Step 7 — Review before scale

Complete `templates/08-pilot-review-scorecard.md`.

Decision options:

- Stop
- Revise and rerun
- Continue limited pilot
- Expand carefully
- Scale with controls
